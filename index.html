Chapter 07

Fusion Compatibility Matrix

Molecular compound harmony scores between flavor families. Based on shared volatile compound groups — flavors that share chemistry = synergistic fusions.

Science of Pairing
Shared Compound Harmony

Chocolate + Coffee share pyrazines (roasted ring compounds). Strawberry + Vanilla share methyl anthranilate (ester family). Pistachio + Rose share terpenes (pinene, geraniol). When flavors share volatile families, each amplifies the other — this is molecular synergy.

Contrast Principle

The best fusions have both harmony AND contrast. Salted caramel: caramel (sweet) × salt (suppresses bitterness receptor). Chocolate orange: bitter cocoa × bright limonene (cuts through fat coating). Mango chili: sweet ester × capsaicin TRPV1 activation. Contrast creates dimension.
